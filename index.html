Professor Elena Prokhorova

Professor Prokhorova studies Soviet and post-Soviet television, visual representations of national identity, and genre and gender theory. She teaches courses in Russian language, and literary and cultural courses on 19th and 20th century Russian literare, media and communism, and contemporary Russian television.
